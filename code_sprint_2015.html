GeoNode 2.4!

Release!

From the 2nd to the 4th of February, users and developers will sit together to make the final release of the version 2.4.

Everyone can help by giving suggestions, write documentation or develop.

The venue in DC is @18F's office (https://18f.gsa.gov/) within the General Service Administration (GSA)

2.5+ roadmap

What's next?

On the 5th everyone will sit together to define what feature GeoNode 2.5+ should have.

This will be an open discussion with the goal of come out with GNIPs and tickets that has to be addresses before the next release.

The Venue @Department of Energy HQ (1000 Independence Ave SW, DC)
